Hi, I'm Darlena!

I'm a designer with both visual and interaction design skills. Visual designers very much own the "look and feel" of a product, which I believe is an important aspect to product design as well. Interaction designers own the flow of the product. I don't believe that one is better without the other. The marriage of the two disciplines is what I believe results in a truly great end-to-end product.

That being said, I'm looking for a role that is more focused on interaction design. While I appreciate and am capable of visual design, time flies when I'm working with the nuances and intracacies of a problem. I want to understand the ins-and-outs to find the right solution. I enjoy working in the constraints of time and development resources. I'm a problem solver that enjoys working in a constrained environment. Figuring out where to push my design boundaries and where energy towards a battle is better fought elsewhere.
